Our Story

More Than a Store

ABS Retail Services LLC was born from a simple idea: families shouldn't have to visit five different stores to get everything they need. We bring together cleaning essentials, clothing, batteries, and fresh groceries under one roof — with the kind of personal service that big-box retailers forgot about.

Our Mission

To make everyday shopping simple and affordable — from trusted cleaning brands and durable clothing to reliable batteries and fresh groceries.

Our Vision

To become Tampa's most trusted neighborhood retailer — known for unbeatable variety, fair pricing, and a shopping experience that feels like visiting a friend.

Our Values

Honesty in every price tag. Quality in every product. Respect for every customer. Great service isn't a luxury — it's the standard.
