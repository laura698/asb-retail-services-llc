About Us

We are a multiservice store committed to offering quality appliances, auto parts & groceries — everything your home and vehicle need, all in one place.

Our Mission:

At OMC Multiservice LLC, we exist to simplify the lives of our customers by offering everything they need under one roof — from home appliances and auto parts to everyday groceries. We partner with trusted global brands to guarantee quality, durability, and fair prices on every product we carry.

More than a store, we are a community ally. We provide personalized advice so every customer finds exactly what they need, whether it's a new refrigerator for the kitchen, a windshield for the car, or pantry essentials for the week — all backed by dedicated after-sales service.

Our Vision:

To become the leading multiservice retailer in Florida — recognized for convenience, competitive pricing, and an unmatched product selection. We are committed to evolving with the latest technology, sustainable practices, and customer-first innovation so that every household and vehicle is equipped with the very best.

Our Values:

  • Integrity. We operate with honesty and transparency in every transaction — no surprises, no hidden costs.
  • Excellence. Every product is carefully selected and verified to meet the highest quality standards.
  • Customer commitment. Your satisfaction drives every decision we make, from product selection to after-sales support.
  • Innovation. We stay ahead of market trends to bring you the latest technology and the best deals available.
  • Community. We proudly serve the Riverview community and are committed to building lasting relationships with our neighbors.
